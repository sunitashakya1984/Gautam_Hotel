Description

The Mithila region is known for its wide variety of tasty cuisine that is marked by a rare simplicity, and The Mithila Kitchen at Hotel Paawan Mithila by Kalash Hospitality strives to excel in this art, as well, besides offering the very best of international cuisine. We aim to satisfy your craving for tasteful and fulfilling meals at all hours of the day.
